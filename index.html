What We Do

HVAC Services Built for West Texas Weather

AC Repair & Summer Service

Compressors, capacitors, refrigerant leaks, blower issues—we troubleshoot electrical and refrigeration problems so your home cools evenly when Lubbock heat spikes.

Heating Repair & Furnace Service

Ignitors, flame sensors, heat exchanger inspections, and gas valve testing—safe, efficient heat for those High Plains cold snaps.

New System Installation & Replacement

Load calculation–driven sizing, quality sheet metal, and startup commissioning so your replacement AC or furnace delivers the efficiency printed on the nameplate.

Preventive Maintenance & Tune-Ups

Seasonal cleanings, coil treatment, electrical draw checks, and filter review—fewer surprise breakdowns and lower operating costs year-round.

Safety & Performance Inspections

Carbon monoxide screening where applicable, temperature splits, static pressure snapshots, and documentation that proves correct, safe operation before you buy or sell.

Ductwork, Airflow & Indoor Comfort

Leaky plenums, restricted returns, and poor balance rob capacity—we seal, repair, and rebalance so conditioned air reaches every room.

Thermostats, Zoning & Smart Controls

Pro-grade stats, wiring repairs, Wi-Fi setup, and zoning that actually matches how your family uses the house—not a generic big-box install.

Emergency HVAC Response

After-hours support for no-heat or no-cool situations—priority triage, honest guidance on safety, and fast parts replacement when Lubbock weather will not wait.
